Kia Ora!

Hello, I’m Jason. You'll recognise me as the tall and mostly introverted person — always wearing a black t-shirt. I’m a designer transitioning from the insurance industry to the tech (or tech-adjacent!) industry.

A good way to describe what I do is that I solve problems for people and businesses. I do that thoughtfully, creatively and pragmatically. I'm comfortable approaching a wide range of problems and the more ambiguity the better! I love bringing clarity to a little bit of chaos.

I’m motivated by mission driven work, aligned values and a welcoming, and inclusive workplace culture. I adamantly believe in working on important problems that meaningfully improve peoples lives.

All in all, I’m a team player, empathetic and good under pressure. I get stuff done and I’m a quick learner with knowledge in lots of areas. I’d love to learn more about how we can work together — and how I can contribute to you and your team.
